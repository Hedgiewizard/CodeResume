The Meaning of Birds by Jaye Robin Brown

To all the folks watching Heartstopper you should read this book. It is a rich queer story about dealing with the pains of losing your first love. I personally really love this book.

I am Princess X by Cherie Priest

Once upon a time, May and Libby met and created Princess X. Libby would draw the pictures and May would write the stories. Once upon a few years later, when Libby was on her way home with her mother, their car crashed into the lake and Libby was presumed dead taking Princess X with her. Once upon a now, years after Libby's presumed death May finds a sticker, a website and mystery that brings into question May's death.
I first read this book when I was yonger(give or take 12) and really loved it. I ended up reading it again years later and loved it even more. This book get better with every read and you pick up on more and more details.
